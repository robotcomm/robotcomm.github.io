Find the Main Menu

On-campus

  1. Press the Messages key (Cisco VoIP phone) OR
    Press the star (*) key and then 0 (analog VoIP phone).
  2. Shared mailbox users only. All others, skip to step 3.

    1. When greeting begins, press the star (*) key.
    2. Enter your 7-digit personal mailbox number (ex., 368-XXXX or 888-XXXX).
    3. Press the pound (#) key.
  3. Enter your PIN (default is 10900).
  4. Press the pound (#) key.

Off-campus

  1. Dial +1-216-754-3800
  2. When prompted for your ID, enter 7-digit personal mailbox number (ex., 368-XXXX or 888-XXXX).
  3. Press the pound (#) key.
  4. Enter your PIN (default is 10900).
  5. Press the pound (#) key.

Click the blue circles to expand menu options to the right. In general, the pattern is "press NUMERIC KEY to perform Action".
