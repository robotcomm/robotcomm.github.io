Access your voice messages

Access your voice messages on or off-campus from any telephone.

Please note: If you are accessing voicemail for the first time after 01/07/2015, use the default passcode: 10900. To protect your privacy, use the instructions at case.edu/its/uc to set a new passcode immediately.

From your desk phone

  1. Press the Messages button
  2. Enter your five-digit passcode and press #.
  3. The default passcode is 10900.

From an analog VoIP phone

  1. Press *, 0.
  2. Enter your five-digit passcode and press #.
  3. The default passcode is 10900.

From any other phone

  1. Call your own 10-digit number (e.g., 216-368-0000).
  2. When the greeting begins to play, press *.
  3. Enter your five-digit passcode and press #.
  4. The default passcode is 10900.
