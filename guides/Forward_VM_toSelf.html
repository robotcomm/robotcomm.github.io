Get ready for Unified Communications

Save important voicemail by forwarding to your email inbox prior the installation of the new voicemail system.(1) Voicemail from the current system will not be retained.

Step 1. Press Messages (2)

Step 2. Enter your voicemail PIN

Step 3. While your message is playing, press 2

Step 4. Enter your 7-digit mailbox number (e.g., 368-1111)

Step 5. When you hear your name press #, 1, 1

Step 6. Repeat for additional messages



1    An additional copy of each voicemail you forward will be sent to both your voicemail and email inboxes. The blinking red light on your phone is one indicator that you have completed the process successfully. Emailed copies of voicemail is easily distinguished by the subject line,

"Case Voicemail: from ######### - ## seconds"

For additional help with Gmail search, filters, labels and other organizational conveniences, please contact the ITS Service desk at (216) 368-HELP or mail uc-info@case.edu.


2   After pressing messages or dialing in to your message inbox,

     * Press 1, 1 to listen to new messages
     * Press 1, 2 to listen to saved messages
