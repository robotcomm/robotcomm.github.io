1. Join the Second Life membership (Getting Start PDF)
    • Go to www.secondlife.com. Click on the large button that says "Free Membership, Join Now"
    • Follow on-screen directions to create your account and password
  2. Download the Second Life Application

    After you have created your account, you will be prompted to download the program.

  3. Create an avatar

    When you first run the program from your computer, you will be prompted to enter your avatar's name (NOT your real-life name) and your password. Your avatar's name was setup when you joined the Second Life membership.

  4. Orientation Island

    Walk through the Orientation Island guide to acquaint yourself with the environment. The guide is simple and appears automatically on the left hand side of your screen. Develop the skills needed in Second Life:

    • Move - basic movement skills
    • Appearance - change your appearance and buy clothes.
    • Search - gives you practice using the search function in
    • Communicate - practice the all-important skill of chatting.
  5. Join the ClevelandPlus Group

    Once you have gone through Orientation Island, come to the ClevelandPlus sim;

    1. Go to the Edit menu and choose Groups.
    2. The Group dialog box opens.
    3. Click the blue search button and type ClevelandPlus (one word) in the search box.
    4. Click Search.
    5. Click ClevelandPlus and its information box opens
  6. Visit the Case Campus on Second Life

    Once you have been joined to the 'ClevelandPlus" group, click on the Map button at the bottom of the screen. When the Map dialog box opens just type in ClevelandPlus in the Group section and click search. Choose the sim called ClevelandPlus (without any Roman numerals). From here you can teleport to the ClevelandPlus sim. Once you are there it would be best to create a landmark that will become part of your inventory.

    System requirement can be found at http://secondlife.com/support/system-requirements/?lang=en-US
