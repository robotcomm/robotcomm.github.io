Quick Reference Guide - Cisco Jabber Guest

Description

Cisco Jabber Guest (Jabber Guest) allows users to join Cisco TelePresence calls from a desktop or laptop computer. Jabber Guest will not allow users to present to meeting attendees. However, users participate fully in the TelePresence meeting in all other respects.

Prerequisites

  • Internet-connected laptop or desktop computer
  • Webcam and microphone
  • Installed browser plugin or extension

Setup

Complete these steps ONCE to use Guest Jabber with selected browser.

  1. Your meeting organizer will provide a link for the TelePresence session.
  2. Prior to the start of the meeting, click the link to install browser extension or plugin.
  3. Your browser will update automatically.
    Note: Depending on chosen browser and operating system, you may be prompted to download a separate installation file. If this is the case, choose a save location and download the file. After download is complete, double-click the file to open it and complete installation.
  4. Allow your browser to restart.
  5. Your browser tabs, including the Jabber Guest session, should restore automatically. If not, click the link provided by your meeting organizer to re-open it.
  6. Click the gear icon in the lower-right corner to update settings.



  7. Choose your speakers, microphone and camera.



  8. Click the gear icon again to return to the main screen.
  9. Jabber Guest is now ready to use.

Join a Meeting

  1. Click the meeting link provided by your meeting organizer.
  2. The Jabber Guest interface opens.
  3. When your are ready to join the meeting, click the green button marked Call.
