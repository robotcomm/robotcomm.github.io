Cisco WebEx™
Meeting Center

Meeting Center allows Web-based conferencing in a collaborative session sharing audio, video, and the desktop. As many as 1,000 participants may attend online meetings by telephone, computer or using a mobile device.

Note: You may be prompted to download a browser extension to use this application.

Schedule a Meeting

  1. Go to cwru-students.webex.com.
  2. Sign in using your CWRU network ID and password.
  3. On the left of the page, click Host a Meeting.

Schedule an Open-ended Meeting

Set up meeting rooms for a project, course, consultation or office hours. This option is useful when the duration or frequency of meeting isn't certain.

  1. Click Schedule a Meeting.

     

  2. Click Advanced
  3. Add a topic (ex., Office Hours, ENG 510).
  4. Uncheck the option marked "Delete from My Meetings when completed" (selected by default).
  5. Click "Return to Quick Scheduler".
  6. Set a future start time.
  7. Add attendees by email (separate emails with a comma or semicolon).
  8. Optionally, set a password.
  9. Click Schedule Meeting.
  10. Attendees will receive an invitation by email.
  11. The organizer may re-open a closed meeting room at any time by clicking Host a Meeting > My Meetings and selecting the meeting by name from the list.
  12. Click Start to restart and refer attendees to previous invite, send a new invite from the application, or copy and paste the Meeting link into an IM or email message.

Schedule Future Meeting

  1. Click Schedule a Meeting.
  2. Add a topic, meeting date, time and duration.
  3. Optionally, set a password.
  4. Add attendees by email (separate emails with a comma or semicolon).
  5. Click Schedule Meeting.
  6. Attendees will receive an invitation by email.

Schedule Meeting for Another User

First, the WebEx account holder should set his or her preferences to specify delegated host(s).

  1. Click Setup > Preferences > Scheduling Options.
  2. Name hosts by email (separate emails with a comma or semicolon).
  3. OR, to choose host(s) from a list, click "Select host". Choose one or more host email addresses and click OK.
  4. Click Save to store your preferences.

A delegated user may then schedule meetings on another's behalf using the Advanced Scheduler:

  1. Click Host a meeting > Schedule meeting > Advanced Scheduler.
  2. Choose the person for whom you are scheduling a meeting from the "Schedule for" dropdown menu.
  3. Schedule a meeting and add attendees as normal.

Start an Ad-hoc Meeting using your 'Personal Room'

Most host accounts include a Personal Room (PR). Personal Rooms allow you to hold instant meetings at any time with the same meeting settings and join link.

The links for Personal Rooms are formatted as follows:

  • Host's Personal Room Web Page: cwru-students.webex.com/meet/UserID
  • Personal Room Join link: cwru-students.webex.com/join/UserID
    Note: This joins you directly into the Personal Room meeting, bypassing the web page.

Add invitees from the WebEx homepage:

  1. Click My Personal Room.
  2. Click Enter Room.
  3. The WebEx application launches.
  4. Click Invite & Remind.

     

  5. Add Invitees by email or phone and click Send. Or, click Invite with your local email to launch your email client (e.g., Outlook, Mac Mail, Thunderbird).
  6. Recipients receive an invitation to join meeting.

Attend Meetings

If you are invited to attend a WebEx meeting, you will receive an email prior to the beginning of the meeting from the meeting organizer. This email contains important meeting details like the date and time of the meeting, call-in instructions and a link to join the meeting online.

Many email clients and webmail applications, including CWRU webmail, allow you to create calendar events directly from email messages.

Wireless connections can be used, but how well they will work depends on the connection speed and your current configuration. If you have issues using WebEx on a wireless internet connection, try using a computer with wired network connection before contacting the Service Desk for technical support.

Attend from a mobile device

To attend a WebEx meeting from your mobile device you'll need to download and install the Cisco WebEx Meetings application for your platform. Cisco supports Android, iOS, Blackberry 10 and Windows Phone 8.

If you have a WebEx account you may view your schedule of upcoming meetings on your device. However, you do not need a WebEx account to attend a meeting.

 

Android

Install application

  1. Search for Cisco WebEx Meetings in the Google Play Store.
  2. Tap Install and follow the on-screen instructions.

View upcoming meetings

  1. Open the WebEx Meetings application on your device, then tap Log In.
  2. Enter your credentials at the Single Sign-On (SSO) page.

Join a meeting or session
  • Without WebEx account
    1. Open the application and tap Join by number.
    2. Enter your meeting or session number.
    3. Enter your name and email address. Then, tap Join.
    4. Or open the invitation email and tap the Join WebEx Meeting.
  • With WebEx account
    When signed in, choose the meeting in the My Meetings view, then tap Join.

iOS

Install application

  1. Search for WebEx in the App Store.
  2. Choose "Cisco WebEx Meetings" and then tap Get to install.

View upcoming meetings

  1. Open the WebEx Meetings application on your device, then tap Sign In.
  2. Enter your credentials at the Single Sign-On (SSO) page.

Join a meeting or session
  • Without WebEx account
    1. Open the application and enter your meeting or session number.
    2. Enter your name and email address. Then, tap Join Meeting.
    3. Or open the invitation email and tap the meeting URL.
  • With WebEx account
    When signed in, choose the meeting in the My Meetings view, then tap Join Meeting.

Attend by telephone (audio only)

  1. Call conference number provided in invitation email.
  2. When prompted, enter meeting access code using telephone keypad.
  3. A tone will indicate that you have entered the meeting.

Attend with computer

  1. Click the meeting link provided by the organizer (e.g., https://cwru-students.webex.com/join/CWRUnetID)
  2. If you have not previously attended a WebEx meeting, the system will prompt you to download and install the Meeting Center application.
  3. The WebEx application will launch and you will join the meeting room.
  4. Click Call Using Computer to connect meeting audio.

     

    Or, click the three dots below to show other audio options and directions:

    • Call Me (the meeting will call you)
    • I Will Call In (click to show instructions)
    • Call Using Computer (default)
  5. When audio has connected successfully, the icon will turn from blue to green and text will read "Connected to Audio".

     

  6. To avoid audio feedback, use headphones or mute microphone when not speaking.
  7. A list of meeting attendees as well as video & audio options are shown in upper-right of the application window.

Attend with computer using telephone for audio

  1. Click the meeting link provided by the organizer (e.g., https://cwru-students.webex.com/join/CWRUnetID)
  2. If you have not previously attended a WebEx meeting, the system will prompt you to download and install the Meeting Center application.
  3. The WebEx application will launch and you will join the meeting room.
  4. A list of meeting attendees as well as video & audio options are shown in upper-right of the application window.
  5. Call conference number provided in invitation email.
  6. When prompted, enter meeting access code and participant number using telephone keypad.
  7. A tone will indicate that you have entered the meeting.
