Make Calls

Start a Jabber to Jabber Call

Please note the following caveats of Jabber to Jabber calling:

  • You can make a Jabber to Jabber call with only one contact at a time.
  • When you are on a Jabber to Jabber call, if you call another contact your ongoing call will end.
This feature must be enabled by your administrator.

To make a call to another Cisco Jabber client:

  1. Choose the call icon for your contact. For example in the contact list or in the chat window.
  2. Choose Jabber Call from the menu.

Change Audio Devices

  1. Plug in your headset or other audio device to the appropriate USB port.
  2. Choose Jabber > Preferences > Audio/Video.
  3. Choose your audio device from the drop-down lists after the hardware is recognized.
  4. Adjust the volume for the audio device.
  5. Close the Preferences window to apply the changes.

Create Ad-hoc Conference Call

You can create an ad-hoc conference with your contacts, simply merge your calls to create a conference call.

  1. Start a call with your first contact.
  2. Start a second call with another contact, the first call is automatically put on hold.
  3. Choose More > Merge and the two calls are merged into a conference call.

Forward Calls

To forward calls from Cisco Jabber:

  1. Choose Jabber > Preferences > Calls.
  2. Choose Call Forward.
  3. In the Forward Calls to section, Choose an option from the available list or add a new number.
  4. To add a new number in the available list, click the + (plus) button.
  5. Enter a phone number, including country and area codes.

URI Dialing

You can use URI dialing to make calls and resolve contacts with Uniform Resource Identifiers (URI).

Users connected to Cisco TelePresence Video Communication Server (VCS), are only accessible using an associated URI.

Setup SIP URI

To create a SIP URI for contacts in your Mac address book:

  1. Choose the user in your Mac address book and click Edit.
  2. Within the phone section of the contact card, add the SIP URI for your contact.
  3. Click Done.
  4. Repeat for additional contacts.

Dialing a Contact using a SIP URI

  1. Enter the characters of the URI including the @ symbol and at least two characters after the @. For example aaa123@ca. The call bricklet will display the results.
  2. Choose Call in the bricklet, to call the contact using their SIP URI.

Transfer an Active Call

  1. Choose the more menu icon and click Transfer Call.
  2. Search for the person you are transferring the call to.
  3. Click the Transfer icon and the call is transferred.

Contacts

Dial from your address book (Mac)

To dial contacts using Cisco Jabber directly from your Mac Address Book, first configure the Mac Address Book Plug-in.

  1. Choose Jabber > Install Mac Address Book Plug-in.
  2. Choose Jabber > Preferences > General > Enable Mac Address Book integration.
  3. Restart the client and the change will take effect.

Add Directory Groups into your contact list

To add a directory group into your contact list:

  1. Choose Jabber > Contacts > New Directory Group.
  2. Enter the name of the directory group.
  3. Choose OK.

Availability

Customize personal status messages and tell your contacts what you are doing at a glance.

  1. Choose the status message field on the main window.
  2. Choose either;
    • Custom Available
    • Custom Away
    • Custom Do Not Disturb
    • Edit Status Message
  3. Enter a message to display.
  4. Press the Return key on your keyboard.
  5. Cisco Jabber displays your personal status message.

Chat

Start a Group Chat

  1. Choose Jabber > File > New Group Chat.
  2. Enter the contacts you want to invite in the People field.
  3. Enter the subject for your group chat.
  4. Click Invite.

Transfer Files

  1. In a chat window, click the file transfer icon.
  2. Choose the file to transfer.
  3. Click Send.

Set Privacy options

You can specify privacy options to control who can see your status and send you messages. When you block a contact, that person cannot view your availability status or send you instant messages. To review and set privacy options:

  1. Choose Jabber > Preferences > Privacy.
  2. Choose your privacy preferences in the Policies section.
  3. Close the Preferences window to apply the changes.

Chat Rooms

When you are added as a member to a chat room, you can participate in the persistent chat room and view the discussion history.

  1. Choose the Chat rooms tab. All the chat rooms that you are a member of are listed.
  2. Double-click title of the room that you want to enter.
  3. View the current participant list on the right side of the chat window.
  4. Read the discussion and enter your own chat messages. The history of the conversation is persistent. When you exit the chat room and close the client, you can return to the conversation in that room. When you return to the chat room, you can view the messages that were sent by other members while you were not logged in.
  5. From the chat room, click Show More to view older messages.

Search Chat Rooms

You can search for instances of keywords in your chat rooms. Only rooms that you are a member of will be searched.

  1. Enter a search term in the client Search field.
  2. Choose Search chat room messages option from the list. A search results window is displayed.
  3. Click Enter select a chat room from the results, or click advanced search to refine search.
