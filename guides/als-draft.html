Video Input Control Panel Quick Start Guide

Quickly and simply configure an Active Learning Space classroom.

 

Turn room on, Display Video Source

  1. Touch the control panel to activate it.
  2. Choose a presentation source from the options shown at the bottom of the control touch panel. Swipe left or right to show more options.
  3. Drag your selected source to the display(s) to start display it.
  4. If you're using a laptop or external source, connect it now.
  5. A different source may be shown on any display.
    NOTE: Controls for some devices, e.g. document camera and Apple TV, may be accessed using the icon in the lower left corner of the control panel).
  6. To show the same source on all displays, drag the source to the "all displays" target.
  7. If control panel does not respond to your touch, try again, but more slowly. Tap and hold for 3 seconds.
  8. To turn the room off, find the control labelled Quit/Shutdown and drag it to "all displays".
  9. Call the ITS Service Desk at (216) 368-HELP (4357) if you need additional help.
