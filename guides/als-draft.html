Active Learning Spaces Quick Start Guide

Quickly and simply configure an Active Learning Space classroom for nearly any purpose in 3 steps.

 

Turn the room on

  1. Using the control touch panel, find the power icon or button labelled Start and tap it.
  2. Individual input devices (e.g., document camera, media players, etc.) power on or return from standby when selected as input.

     

    NOTE: To turn the room off, find the control labelled Quit/Shutdown and tap it.

     

     

Prep for presentation

  1. Choose a presentation source from the options shown at the bottom of the control touch panel.
  2. Swipe left or right to show more options.
  3. If you're using a laptop or external source, connect it now.
  4. Drag your selected source to the front display(s) to start the projection system.

     

    NOTE: Projector screen is represented at the top of the control panel and distinguished by the label "main" (main left and main right for rooms with dual projection).

     

     

  5. Optionally, to set a presenter monitor, drag your presentation source to a pod at the bottom and center of control touch panel. Make sure that to face the selected pod toward the front of the room.

Share your content

  1. Choose a presentation source from the options shown at the bottom of the control touch panel.
  2. Swipe left or right to show more options.
  3. Touch the device name to select it, then drag it to the station where you want it to display.

     

     

  4. To assign a source to all pods simultaneously, drag to source to the box labelled ALL PODS.
  5. To assign a source to all pods simultaneously and also display the same on the front projection screen(s), drag the source to the box labelled ALL DISPLAYS.
