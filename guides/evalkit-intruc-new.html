EvaluationKit

To begin, navigate to case.evaluationkit.com. Sign in with your CWRU Network ID and password if prompted.

Step 1: Choose and Add Custom Survey Questions

  1. Click the white link at the top of the page named Attach Surveys to Projects.
  2. Find the name of the appropriate project in the list that appears and click the blue + icon in the "Add Questions" column.
  3. On the page that opens ("Custom Question Survey") click the blue link named + Create New Survey.
  4. Add a title and description. Then click Save New.
  5. Choose Item Bank Question from the "Select Question Type" dropdown menu.
  6. Choose Faculty Senate Question Bank from the "Item Bank Survey" dropdown menu and click Get Questions to continue.
  7. Choose one or more questions from those presented onscreen by selecting the checkbox above the question text. Then click Add Selected.
  8. Review the new custom survey on the next page and click Save.

Step 2: Associate Custom Questions with the Main Survey

  1. Click the white link at the top of the page named Attach Surveys to Projects.
  2. Find the name of the appropriate project in the list.
  3. Click the blue + icon in the "Add Questions" column.
  4. On the page that opens ("Custom Question Survey") click the blue link named + Add Custom Question Survey.
  5. Click the blue ✓ icon in the "Select" column to choose your survey. Then click Select Courses to continue.

Step 3: Add Recipients by Course

  1. Click Add Courses to search for courses that should receive the additional Custom Survey Questions.
  2. Search for courses using Title, Code or Unique ID.
  3. Choose appropriate courses from the results list, and click Add Selected Courses.
  4. To complete, click Finish: Save Custom Question Survey.
