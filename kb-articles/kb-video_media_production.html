Video and Media Production

The MediaVision Production group offers professional production services to the faculty, staff and students of Case Western Reserve University. Our team works in all areas of production, from single camera capturing to large scale media production and rich data collaborations that meet a multitude of needs and expectations. Our support goes beyond the normal broadcast standards: we have taken productions into the virtual world of Second Life, through the hearts and minds of global dignitaries and into the classrooms of our own renowned faculty and students. Contact us at 216.368.3777 or case.edu/its/mediavision/.
