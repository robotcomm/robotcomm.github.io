Using RAVE with Mobile Phones

Procedure

Rave can be updated using your mobile smartphone. Point your mobile browser to www.getrave.com/login/cwru

If you are already signed into single sign-on, the Rave page appears. If you are not you will be directed to the CWRU Single Signon page to enter your CWRU Network ID and password. After logging in the Rave page will appear.

The RaveAlert page appears.

Proceed to update per the instructions listed for user profiles, mobile phone, voice phone or email.

Add Rave Alerts to your contacts to easily identify text or calls from RaveAlert in an emergency.

  1. Add a new contact
  2. Use the phone number 22677
  3. Give the contact a name such as CWRU Alerts
