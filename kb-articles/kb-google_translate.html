Google Translate basics

To translate words and phrases instantly, select your translation language and start typing. The translation result should appear instantly as you type. You can always click the Translate button to trigger a translation.

When you translate a single word, you may see a simple dictionary at the bottom indicating parts of speech and possible word variations. For most language pairs, you'll also see a View Detailed Dictionary link. This will display a more detailed Google Dictionary page with example sentences, images and more.

If you are not sure exactly what language you are attempting to translate, the Detect language option can help you figure this out. The accuracy of the automatic language detection function increases with the amount of text entered.

For additional information on Google Translate features, click this link to visit the Google Translate Help website.
