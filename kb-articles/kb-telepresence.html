TelePresence Services

The Cisco TelePresence videoconference unit in room 124 of Tomlinson Hall enables users to communicate with other, geographically distributed TelePresence users through high-quality, lifelike video. Its high speed connection to the World Wide Web is enabled through a direct connection to the National Lambda Rail.

For more information or to reserve the TelePresence room, contact the Office of International Affairs at 216.368.2397. To schedule a TelePresence call, please contact the ITS Service Desk at 216.368.HELP (4357).
