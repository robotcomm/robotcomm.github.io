Definitions

Centrex Analog

Centrex telephone lines are really just normal telephone lines which provide extended services, typically including:

  • Three-way calling for conference calls
  • Call transfer (ability to transfer a call to any telephone number)
  • Caller ID
  • Voice mail

VoIP Analog

Use to connect traditional analog telephones, fax machines, Talk Box, emergency telephones, elevator phones and other similar customer-premises devices to a digital telephone system or a voice over IP telephony network.

VoIP

Voice over IP (VoIP) is a methodology and group of technologies for the delivery of voice communications and multimedia sessions over Internet Protocol (IP) networks, such as the Internet. Other terms commonly associated with VoIP are IP telephony, Internet telephony, broadband telephony, and broadband phone service.

VoIP is used in conjunction with Cisco Model Telephones on campus.

Where is my MAC Address located?

The MAC Address can be located on the bottom of your Cisco Phone. The MAC Address sticker will be designated by the work MAC in a raised format next to the correct sticker. The MAC address is always a 12 digit sequence.
