Webcasting

Web casting is the method of using streaming video to send data over the Internet in real time. Through the use of a player (such as Real Player or Windows Media Player), users can view video clips, virtual labs, lectures and other Interactive materials either through pre-recorded video clips or live broadcast feeds.

For more information, see case.edu/its/mediavision/video-conferencing-types/.
