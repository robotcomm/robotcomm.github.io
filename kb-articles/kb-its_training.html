ITS Training

Faculty, staff and students may obtain training and consultation support for a wide range of topics and services to help you adopt our innovative educational and enterprise technologies. Please call 216.368.HELP (4357) or email help@case.edu to request training and/or consultation services in the areas you desire.

For more information, see help.case.edu.
