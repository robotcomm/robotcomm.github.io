Public Information

The types of information listed below are examples of Public Information. If disclosed, Public Information is considered to have a negligible impact on the university. Tier I Controls apply to systems containing Public Information.

General Information

  • news and public announcements
  • general communications
  • course offerings, in particular material published on CWRU YouTube and iTunesU
  • budget information when approved for disclosure
  • publications when approved for disclosure Employee Information
  • name
  • CWRU Network ID (e.g. abc123)
  • CWRU 7-digit Employee ID or Student ID (also referred to as EmplID)
  • salary (when published in IRS Form 1099)
  • gross pension
  • value/nature of fringe benefits
  • job titles
  • job description
  • educational background, degrees, titles, etc.
  • work (office) location
  • work phone number
  • email address (first.last@case.edu)
  • honors and awards received Student Directory Information
  • name
  • permanent address
  • 7 digit Student ID
  • email address
  • dates of enrollment
  • enrollment status
  • major
  • college
  • class year
  • academic awards and honors
  • degree status (received)
