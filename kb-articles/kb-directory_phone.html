ITS KBA 154948 HCM Phone Update
> ITS > ITS Development > rpretzer > KBA > ITS KBA 154948 HCM Phone Update

Update Campus Directory phone number

  1. Sign in to PeopleSoft Human Capital Management (HCM).
  2. Click the menu labeled Main Menu, then Self Service.
  3. Click Personal Information, then Phone Numbers.
  4. Click the button labeled Add Phone Number.
  5. Choose the phone type Business from the drop-down list and type in the new phone number.
  6. Click the text box in the column labeled Telephone to edit its contents.
  7. Replace the outdated phone number with the new one.
  8. Select the Preferred check box.
  9. Click the button labeled Save. The ERP system will update employee record within 24 hours.
