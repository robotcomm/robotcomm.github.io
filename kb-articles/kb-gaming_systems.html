Gaming Systems

Students are welcome to bring their gaming consoles to campus and use the CWRU network for connecting and playing multi-user online games. The Service Desk and the ITS CARE Center offer general support for personal gaming systems. The ITS Service Desk offers complimentary 24/7/365 support from a technical support staff member by calling 216.368.HELP (4357).

Gaming consoles that utilize the wired network must be registered. Go to case.edu/its/devreg/ to register your console. You can call 216.368.HELP (4357) for assistance.
