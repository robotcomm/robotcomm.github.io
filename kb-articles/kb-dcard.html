DCard Training

The DCard, or Department Card, is administered by Procurement and Distribution Services. To have a DCard in your department, a custodian must be trained to maintain it. The online DCard Training course is one part of the requirements for becoming a DCard custodian.

Use these directions to access DCard training in Adobe Connect.

1.

Log in to connect.case.edu with your CWRU Network ID and password.

  

2.

Click on the Training Catalog tab if it is not already active.

3.

Click on the DCard Training curriculum.

4.

Click the Enroll button.

  

5.

The “Enrollment Approved” message appears. Click the OK button

  

6.

Click the View button to see the

DCard Training curriculum.

  

7.

A new window appears containing the DCard Trainingcurriculum. The user can now begin taking courses.

Once you have enrolled in DCard Training, use these directions to take the classes.

1.

If you aren’t already logged in to Adobe Connect, go to connect.case.edu/dcard

and sign in with your CWRU Network ID and password.

2.

The DCard Training curriculum appears.

  

3.

Courses with a star are required. The Open button will show what courses are available to you. Click the Open button to open the DCard Training course.

  

4.

When you have finished the DCard Training course, close the window in which it appeared. A green checkmark will appear on the course name. The Open button changes to the Review button. Click on the Review button to open the course again and take it as many times as you desire.
