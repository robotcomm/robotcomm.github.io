Stolen Computer

In the event a computer is lost or stolen at CWRU, it is important to first contact Campus Police at 216.368.3333. They will assist the owner in filing a police report and in attempting to locate the stolen device. If the computer is a machine owned by CWRU, the faculty or staff member will need to speak to their supervisor to ensure that a replacement machine is ordered. For student and personally-owned machines, the device owner bears the responsibility of ordering a replacement. Discounts on computers are offered through the eStore.
