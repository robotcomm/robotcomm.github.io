RAVE: Adding Voice Phone

Procedure

If available, your CWRU phone number will be in the Voice Only Line Contacts section. You may add up to 3 additional phone numbers.

 

 

Step

Action

1.

Click the Add link.

 

 

Step

Action

2.

Enter the phone number into the Phone Number field. It may be entered with or without dashes. Leave the Extension field blank.

3.

Click the Save link.

 

 

 

Step

Action

4.

The new number appears in the Voice Online Line Contacts section. Using the buttons next to the number you may edit or delete.

 

5.

This completes how to enter your voice only number in the Rave system.

End of Procedure.
