Desktop Email Notifications

Email notifications for Google Mail can be turned on or off through both Google Mail settings in webmail.case.edu and the Google Talk instant messaging client.

Google Mail

Step Action
1. Log in to your Google Mail at webmail.case.edu
2. Click the Option Settings button.
3. Click the Mail Settings link.
4.

 

 

 

 

Locate the Desktop Notifications section.
5. Select one of the following options:
  • New mail notifications on
  • Important mail notifications on
  • Mail notifications off
6. Click the Save Changes button at the bottom of the screen.

Google Talk Client

Step Action
1. Open and sign in to the Google Talk client.
2. Click the Settings link.
3. The Settings window appears. Click the Notifications item on the left side of the window.
4. Select the appropriate settings for the desktop notifications you wish to receive from Google Talk.
5. If the Show Notification checkbox is selected under New Email, a popup will appear on your desktop every time Google Talk is open and a new email arrives.
6. Click the OK button to save your changes.
