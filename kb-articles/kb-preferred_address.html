Preferred Addresses

If you use a different first name than your given name (e.g. Sandy versus Sandra), you may create a preferred firstname.lastname@case.edu address. Emails sent to or from that address can be accessed from within the same CWRU email account used for your official email address. For more information go to its-services.case.edu/pFDL/.
