OSPARS

OSPARS (Office of Sponsored Projects Accounting Reporting System) contains a series of management summary level reports of Proposals and Awards across the university over a period of 3 to 5 years prior to the current fiscal year. It is supported by the Office of Sponsored Projects at CWRU. For more information, go to ora.ra.case.edu/ospa
