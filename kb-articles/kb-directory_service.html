Directory Service

The contact information for faculty, staff and students is available within the Case Western Reserve University telephone directory. By visiting case.edu/directory, users can search for individuals within CWRU including Alumni listings, departmental listings and the student directory. All information is stored and maintained in compliance with the Acceptable Use Policy.
