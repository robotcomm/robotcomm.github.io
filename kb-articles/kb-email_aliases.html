Email Aliases

You can create email aliases for yourself, an organization, or an event (such as 'falldance@case.edu') through the email alias creator. Using CWRU Google Email, multiple users can use the email alias for the purpose of receiving and sending emails. For more information go to its-services.case.edu/mailalias/
