Introduction to Purchasing at CWRU

If you need to manage requisitions (create or approve), or to create payment requests using PeopleSoft Financials you'll need to submit a security form and complete training.

How to access training

  1. Classroom – Find available classes and register.
  2. Online – Visit connect.case.edu and log in with your CWRU Network ID and password. Choose Purchasing at CWRU from A to Z from the Training Catalog and to complete the online training and take the quiz.

Questions?

Contact the Service Desk at 216.368.HELP (4357) for help with Online Training. Or for assistance with classroom registration email caps@case.edu.
