CWRU Apartments Network and Cable

High-speed wired Internet access in the following CWRU Property Management Apartments is not provided or supported by the university. Adelphia/Time Warner is responsible for providing and servicing the cable modem/router and necessary connectivity to these locations. Please refer to the following websites for additional information.
