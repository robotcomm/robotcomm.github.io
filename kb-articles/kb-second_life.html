Second Life

Case Western Reserve University has a prominent presence in the 3D virtual world of Second Life. Its islands have been carefully constructed to provide teachers, students, researchers and visitors with a variety of learning environments both in and out of the classroom. CWRU also has its own private Second Life environment, when more privacy is required for a project. For more information go to case.edu/its/second-life/.
