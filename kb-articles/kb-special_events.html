Special Events

MediaVision Audio Visual (AV) and Event Support provides traditional audiovisual services for the CWRU community. From classroom and meeting room support to large scale events such a commencement, we can provide a wide array of equipment, personnel and technical services to meet your needs. Our goal is to provide reliable AV support so our clients can concentrate on other aspects of their class or event. 

If you have questions, please refer to the information included on the MediaVision website or call 216.368.3777.
