Software Center

The Software Center is open to students, staff and paid faculty of Case Western Reserve University. It is the distribution website of software licenses intended to be used on institutional computers of CWRU personnel and student-owned computers. Most titles are free; some are significantly reduced from retail cost.

The Software Center contains titles such as operating system upgrades, Microsoft Office, Symantec Endpoint Solutions antivirus software, and Identity Finder. Symantec and Identity Finder are required to be installed and run on faculty and staff computers.

To access the Software Center, you must be connected to the CWRU wired network (for optimal download speed), the CaseWireless wireless network, or the Virtual Private Network (VPN).
