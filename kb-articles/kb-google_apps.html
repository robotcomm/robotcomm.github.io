Google Apps for Education

Sign in with your CWRU Network ID and password to access Google Apps and Services.

Blogging

  • Blogger: Share your life online with a blog

Business and Productivity

  • Google Contacts: Contacts application; integrates with gmail, calendar, others
  • Calendar: Calendar application; integrates with gmail, tasks, others
  • Google Cloud Print: Manage prints jobs and compatible wireless printers in the cloud
  • Google Drive, Docs, Sheets: Create, edit, share and store documents in the cloud
  • Gmail: Email
  • Google Finance: Business info, news and interactive charts
  • Alerts: Customized email updates on topics of your choice
  • Google Tables: Import and visualize table data online; integrates with Google Drive and Docs
  • Google Sites: Build and share websites
  • Google Moderator: Gather and prioritize questions or opinions on any topic from a group of people

Conversations

  • Google Hangouts: Video calling, voice chat, IM
  • Google Groups: Use Google Groups

Everything Else

  • Google Play Store: Purchase digital media, apps for Android devices, computing hardware and more
  • Chrome Web Store: Extend Chrome, Drive, Sheets and Docs, purchase web applications developed by Google and 3rd parties

Help with Google Products

  • Google Help Forums: Get questions answered and read popular discussions in Google support communities
  • Support.google.com: Product support provided by Google

Maps

  • Google Maps: View maps and directions
  • Google Map Maker: Become a citizen cartographer and help map your world
  • Panoramio Geolocate: store, organize and map your photographs
  • Google Earth: View satellite imagery, maps, terrain, 3D buildings and more

Language Tools

  • Google Translate: View web pages in other languages
  • Google Translator Toolkit: Tools to translate your pages and documents

Search

  • Google Search: Search the web
  • Google News: Search thousands of news stories
  • Google Patent Search: Search through the US Patent database
  • Google Images: Search for images
  • Google Blog Search: Search just blogs
  • Web History: View and search the full text of the pages you've visited
  • Google Flights: Search flight times and fares
  • Google Scholar: Search scholarly articles and abstracts

Social

  • Google Bookmarks: Social bookmarking
  • Google Chrome Sync: Synchronize your bookmarks, browser preferences, and browser theme on multiple computers with Chrome browser
  • Google Plus: Social networking

Under the Hood

  • Google Analytics: Measure website traffic and marketing effectiveness
  • Content Experiments (formerly Site Optimizer)
  • Google Code: Write apps for Google and Android
  • Dashboard: View product-specific data associated with your Google Account
  • Webmaster Tools: Detailed reports about webpages visibility on Google

Visual Design and Images

  • Sketchup: Build three-dimensional models
  • 3D Warehouse: Find existing three-dimensional Sketchup models

YouTube

  • YouTube: Watch, upload and share videos
  • YouTube Content Manager: Identify and manage your content with Content ID
  • YouTube for Developers: API access, programmatically manage your content on YouTube
