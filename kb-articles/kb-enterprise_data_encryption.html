Enterprise Data Encryption

The IronKey® is an encrypted USB drive for maintaining and protecting sensitive information in small amounts (less than 4 GB). It can be purchased through the eProcurement system; see your management center for assistance in making the purchase. Data can also be stored on highly secured, highly-available servers through a contract with ITS. Contact help@case.edu for information.
