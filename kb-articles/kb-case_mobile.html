Case Mobile

Case Mobile, developed by Information Technology Services (ITS), is a new way to take CWRU web-based services to the mobile environment. To provide a common user experience, many services found in Case Launchpad are also available through Case Mobile.

To access Case Mobile, point your mobile web browser to http://m.case.edu.

Questions?

For technical issues regarding Case Mobile, please contact the ITS Service Desk at help@case.edu or 216.368.HELP (4357).
