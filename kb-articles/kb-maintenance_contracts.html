Maintenance Contracts

Basic support services are provided to the entire CWRU community at no cost. These services support the fundamental requirements of computing on campus. Support for all applications, such as email and calendaring, are included. View the Technology Service Agreements brochure at Technology Support Agreements brochure

If your department requires additional or specialized services and support, please visit help.case.edu or call 216.368.HELP (4357) and inquire about the additional campus Maintenance Agreements available.
