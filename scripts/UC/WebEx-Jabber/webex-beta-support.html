WebEx Beta Testing

Starting 06/24/2015 Case Western Reserve University will conduct a limited beta test of WebEx Meeting Center and WebEx Training Center with pre-selected members of the campus community.

Any inquiry from a beta tester (self-identified) should be summarized in a ticket and assigned to the WebEx Beta resolver group. Do not attempt to resolve WebEx issues during the beta test period. Although the beta is closed, inquiries from interested users who are not part of the beta group should also be forwarded to the WebEx Beta resolver group.

During the beta period, please include the following pre-formatted response when responding to beta participants by email:
