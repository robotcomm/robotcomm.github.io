How to escalate

Who to contact

  1. Dave Weilacher, dave.weilacher@case.edu, (216) 368-4184 (office)

Description and purpose

An escalation may be requested by an end user when he or she determines that a critical issue has not been acknowledged properly, remains unresolved for an unacceptable period of time or is severe enough that it requires atypical handling.

Escalation should not be confused with assignment to a resolver group. An escalated issue is typically brought to the attention of management within a second-level support group(s).

Escalation procedures

Escalations are available whenever requested, including during planned university closures and recognized university holidays.

During unplanned university closures escalations may NOT be available.

Classify the reason for escalation as one of the following; Confirmed Outage (total or partial), Suspected Outage (total or partial), Abend, Slow response time, User Requirements not met, Other functional or Other technical.

Cannot retrieve message from Communite

back to top

  1. Gather resolution information and enter it into the ticket.
  2. After 01/07/15 the Communite voicemail system will no longer be available.
  3. The replacement voicemail system, Unity Connection, will not retain messages stored in Communite.
  4. To listen to new messages in either system, press 1 at the main menu.
  5. Because Unity Connection will not retain messages stored in Communite, ITS recommends that users produce email copies of important messages by forwarding the message to his or herself prior to the upgrade.
  6. Instructions for this process are included in this document and may also be accessed externally at http://www.case.edu/its/uc/save-voicemail.html.
  7. For additional instructions, refer to voicemail shortcuts.

Archive Communite voicemails in email

back to top

  1. Gather resolution information and enter it into the ticket.

  2. Repeat steps FOR EACH message user will save.

    1. Press messages and enter your passcode
    2. Press 1 to retrieve messages, then 2 to retrieve saved messages.
    3. While the message is playing, press 2 to forward the message
    4. Enter your 7-digit mailbox number (e.g., 368-1111)
    5. When you hear your name press #, 1, 1

  3. Provide user with link to external reference, http://www.case.edu/its/uc/save-voicemail.html.

  4. Resolve ticket.

How do I set a voicemail PIN

back to top

  1. Gather resolution information and enter it into the ticket.
  2. PIN and passcode are terms used interchangeably.
  3. Using desk phone
  4. Press messages button on telephone.
  5. While listening to the main menu, press 5, 5, 3.
  6. The default PIN (passcode) is 10900.
  7. From another phone
  8. Call your telephone number (e.g., (216) 368-1111).
  9. Press * to skip your greeting.
  10. While listening to the main menu, press 5, 5, 3.
  11. From your computer
  12. Navigate to Unity Connection web portal.
  13. Supply your CWRU Network ID and password, when prompted.
  14. Select Options > PIN
  15. Enter current or default PIN.
  16. Set new PIN.
  17. Resolve ticket.
