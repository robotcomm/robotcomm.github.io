Escalation

Description and purpose

An escalation may be requested by an end user when he or she determines that a critical issue has not been acknowledged properly, remains unresolved for an unacceptable period of time or is severe enough that it requires atypical handling.

Escalation should not be confused with assignment to a resolver group. An escalated issue is typically brought to the attention of management within a second-level support group(s).

Who to contact for issue escalation

Example, **NO ESCALATION**:
Issue escalation is not offered at this time. All unresolved tickets should be assigned to the XX resolver group.

Example, **TYPICAL ESCALATION** (Break down further for complex applicatoins or interdependent services):
NAME, PREFERRED CONTACT (TYPE), ALTERNATE CONTACT 1 (TYPE), ALTERNATE CONTACT 2 (TYPE)

  1. Joseph Specialist, joe.specialist@case.edu, (216) 123-4567 (mobile), (216) 368-0000 (office)
  2. Janice Manager, janice.manager@case.edu, (216) 123-4567 (mobile), (216) 368-0000 (office)
