Online Engineering Degrees

The online engineering master’s programs will be developed and operated through a partnership between CWRU and Colloquy. For all student-facing communications, Colloquy refers to its operation as the Online Education Center on behalf of Case Western Reserve University. As such, Colloquy operates and appears to students as a virtual extension of CWRU.

Curriculum information and admissions requirements are listed at http://online-engineering.case.edu/

Additional Information

Online Education Center Help Desk

Courses are not hosted in Blackboard. Our vendor partner (Colloquy) will host program courses in a Learning Management System (LMS) called "Learn", a customized version of the open source Moodle system. Colloquy maintains this system and supports administrative, instructor and student access to this application. Colloquy’s (Online Education Center) help desk will support login and password reset for the system. We enroll students in their courses in LEARN after they have registered through SIS.

Colloquy (Online Education Center) will provide help desk support for Learn to students and faculty enrolled in our MS online engineering program courses on a 365/24/7 basis.

Should students enrolled in the online engineering master’s program courses be inadvertently directed to your team for help desk support for Learn LMS, please provide a warm handoff by contacting the CWRU Online Education Center, toll-free at (855) 976-4004 (select option 5, then option 2 for Help Desk).

Technology Systems - CWRU

Students enrolled in online degree programs will have access to the same resources and services as a traditional (on-campus) students.

  • Applicants will apply to the program using CWRU’s Hobsons AY system.
  • Prospective students can learn more about the online degree programs at online-engineering.case.edu or by calling 855.976.4004.
  • CWRU’s SIS (Peoplesoft) will be the system of record for all program students.
