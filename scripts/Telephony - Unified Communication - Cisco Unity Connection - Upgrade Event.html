Set a new PIN

back to top

  1. Press Messages key on office phone.
  2. Enter the default five-digit PIN (10900) and press #
  3. While listening to the main menu, press 5, 3, 1
  4. Enter a new five-digit PIN and press * to confirm it.

Record a new greeting

back to top

After setting PIN, user will be guided through the steps to record a new name and greeting.

To access the Greetings menu
1. Press Messages key on office phone.
1. Enter PIN.
1. While listening to the main menu press 5, 1

To change recorded name
1. Press Messages key on office phone.
1. Enter PIN.
1. While listening to the main menu press 5, 3, 2
