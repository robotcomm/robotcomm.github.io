Service Description

Visualization is the process of rendering information for the purpose of visual appreciation or understanding.

Research Computing —

  • supports visualization activities for researchers throughout the CWRU community
  • provides and supports systems to produce, explore and view visualization materials
  • engages the research community as both consultants and collaborators
  • facilitates training in visualization methods (delivered via seminars and curated online training materials)
  • coordinates with the High-Performance Computing (HPC) user community for the visualization of selected scientific data

An overview of information about visualization support services is available at the Data Visualization google site

Researchers are invited to participate in the growth of research computing at CWRU by providing hardware, or by sharing software and operational costs.

Escalation

An escalation may be requested by an end user when he or she determines that a critical issue has not been acknowledged properly, remains unresolved for an unacceptable period of time or is severe enough that it requires atypical handling.

An escalated issue is typically brought to the attention of management within a second-level support group(s).
