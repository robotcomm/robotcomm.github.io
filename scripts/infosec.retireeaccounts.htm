Service Description

Electronic communication services, including Case Western Reserve email accounts, are maintained primarily to serve the needs of the University community for teaching, research, scholarship and administration.

Qualifying retirees may elect to have email forwarded from a university-owned email account to a personal email account of his or her choosing. Retiree accounts,

  • will not provide functional access to any university systems,
  • may not be construed to imply permissable use of any university system, and
  • must be requested through Human Resources, Benefits Administration.

Email forwarding accounts may be renewed annually through the Benefits Administration. The university may choose to suspend email forwarding service or individual forwarding accounts at any time, at its discretion.

Users with detailed policy questions should refer to the university Acceptable Use Policy (AUP). Please remember that the AUP applies to all faculty, staff, students, alumni, guests, external individuals or organizations and individuals accessing external network services via university facilities.

Use this article when:

  3. Any user requests general information about the scope of the service.

Detailed Troubleshoot / Repair Procedures

return to top | index | troubleshoot questions

User is not receiving CWRU email at forwarding address.

return to troubleshoot questions

  1. Gather resolution information and enter it into the ticket.
  2. Confirm that user has elected to receive email forwarded to a personal account through the Benefits Administration office.
  3. If no, please direct user to contact Benefits Administration.
  4. Confirm that call date is later that user's last day of employment (email forwarding does not begin until after Close of business on retiree's final day.)
  5. Confirm that user has received and replied to forwarding request confirmation.
  6. If no, then have user check Spam/Junk folder of personal email address.
  7. If confirmation has not been received, note that user requests a new confirmation email and forward ticket to Information Security.
  8. Confirm that CWRU account is not forwarding. Send a test email to CWRU account and and confirm that message arrives in user's personal account.
  9. If test message does not arrive, confirm that CWRU Network ID is valid using the ID Lookup Tool. User ID is valid when Affiliate flag is set ("= o").
  10. Note troubleshooting steps taken.
  11. Resolve the ticket or forward to Information Security resolver group.

User did not receive forwarding request confirmation email.

return to troubleshoot questions

  1. Gather resolution information and enter it into the ticket.
  2. Confirm that user has elected to receive email forwarded to a personal account through the Benefits Administration office.
  3. If no, please direct user to contact Benefits Administration.
  4. Direct user to check Spam/Junk folder of personal email address for confirmation request.
  5. Note that user requests a new confirmation email.
  6. Resolve the ticket or forward to Information Security resolver group.
