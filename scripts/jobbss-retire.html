CWRULink replaces JOBBSS

Keywords
JOBBSS; Student employment; Work study; CWRULink; Career Services

CWRULink will be used for all student job listings. CWRULink replaces JOBBSS, which has been retired. If you are unable to answer any question, direct the user to email his or her question to careers@case.edu.

 

FAQ

Reset CWRULink password
Open an Internet browser and go to https://case-csm.symplicity.com/
  1. Select the type of employer you are.
    • Non-faculty employers, select Employer.
    • Faculty employers, select Faculty.
  2. Click the "Forgot Password" link on the Sign in page.

 

For employers

The JOBBSS system isn't available. How do I post a student employment job?
JOBBSS has been retired. The new job posting system for Student Employment is CWRULink.
  1. Open an Internet browser and go to https://case-csm.symplicity.com/
  2. Select the type of employer you are.
    • Non-faculty employers, select Employer. Faculty employers, select Faculty.
    • Sign in or first-time users register for an account.

 

Login to CWRULink.
  • Open an Internet browser and go to https://case-csm.symplicity.com/
  • Your username is your email address, e.g., abc124@case.edu.
  • NOTE: Your password may or may not match your email password.

 

For students

I used to look up Student Employment job postings in JOBBSS, but the system isn't available.
Where do I look for a student employment job (work-study)?
JOBBSS has been retired. The new job posting system for Student Employment is CWRULink.
  1. Open an Internet browser and go to https://case-csm.symplicity.com/
  2. Click "Student/Alumni".
  3. Click the link that says, "If you are a current CWRU student, please login here with your Case Network ID and password."

 

I'm in CWRULink. How do I find Student Employment jobs?
Filter by Position Type for "Student Employment and/or Work-Study". This search will return all job postings for on and off campus student employment, both work-study and non-work study.
