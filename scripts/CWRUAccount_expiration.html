Identity Management | Bulk Account Deletion | Event


Keywords:
CWRU Account; NetworkID; Account access; Account expiration; Account deletion; Bulk delete; Gmail; Google Takeout; Identity Management

Event Description

A group of expired CWRU accounts that remained active in error will be manually removed by ITS on DD MM, YYYY at HH:MM AM/PM. Users will not have access to any CWRU system after account is removed. ITS will begin notifying users of account action 7 days prior to removal. Email notifications will continue until 1 day prior to event.

Escalation or resolvers are NOT provided for these users. Analyst will resolve ticket at:

  1. Close of initial call
  2. After initial email response is sent
  3. Close of initial chat conversation

NOTE: If user is adamant, and can provide a legitimate reason why account should not have expired (e.g., change in role / affiliation / name, etc.), then collect required information (name, CWRU ID, contact information, justification) and assign the ticket to NetworkID.

Permissible Response

Provide users with:

  1. Date and time access will expire
  2. Scope of action: affects access to ALL CWRU ITS services, including Google Applications
  3. Instructions for using Google Takeout to download account data (including email): http://bit.ly/12dgiLg

Do not attempt to:

  1. Explain or justify access past previous expiration date
  2. Propose any workaround or backdoor access (aside from Google Takeout, as noted)
  3. Estimate time for Google Takeout to complete archive (instruct users to begin process as early as feasible)

User notification text:

Your CWRU user account will expire on DD MM, YYYY at HH:MM AM/PM EST. After this time you will no longer be able to access any CWRU ITS services, including all Google Applications (Gmail, Contacts, Calendar, et al.)

Refer to the linked instructions to download a copy of your Gmail data prior to account expiration: http://bit.ly/12dgiLg

As a reminder, CWRU ITS will never ask for sensitive personal information or access credentials (passwords).
