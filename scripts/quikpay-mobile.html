QuikPay Mobile Application

Beginning Friday July 31, 2015, the mobile version of QuikPay will be made available for or students wishing to check their balances, access monthly statements, and make tuition ACH or credit card payments. Users should contact Student Financial Services directly (M-F, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM) at studentaccounts@case.edu to resolve any questions regarding the application.
